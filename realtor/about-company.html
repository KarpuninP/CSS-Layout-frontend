About company

PiterRentPlus was founded in 2012 and offers daily rental of luxury apartments in historical district of St. Petersburg. Our apartments are located within walking distance of Hermitage, Winter Palace, Mariinsky Theater and St. Isaac's Cathedral.

In addition to rent, we offer: transfer from the airport, preparation of reporting documents, cash and non-cash payment, assistance in obtaining visas for foreign residents. We use transitive accommodation system that allows you to book apartments at any time from anywhere with 100% settlement guarantee. And if you change your mind, then you can cancel your reservation without additional fees for 48 hours.
